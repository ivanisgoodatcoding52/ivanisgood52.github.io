Día de Muertos

Día de Muertos, or Day of the Dead, is a Mexican tradition celebrating the lives of the deceased. Families set up ofrendas (altars) with marigold flowers, sugar skulls, candles, and personal items to welcome spirits back for a night of remembrance and love.
